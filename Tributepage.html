Tribute to MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, is a former Indian cricketer and captain known for his strategic mind and calm demeanor. His leadership took India to numerous victories, and he is celebrated as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen in cricket history.

Early Life and Career:

Dhoni began his cricket career in local and domestic circuits, making his international debut in 2004. His impressive performance quickly established him as a key player for India.

Notable Achievements:

  • 2007: Led India to victory in the ICC World Twenty20.
  • 2011: Captained India to win the ICC Cricket World Cup.
  • 2013: Guided India to the ICC Champions Trophy title.
  • 2008-2014: Captained Chennai Super Kings to multiple IPL titles.
